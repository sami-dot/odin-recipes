Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has become a worldwide favorite, known for its layers of rich, flavorful ingredients and comforting appeal. The recipe typically starts with a robust meat sauce made from ground beef or sausage, simmered with onions, garlic, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs like basil and oregano. This savory mixture is then layered between sheets of pasta, along with creamy ricotta cheese, gooey mozzarella, and a sprinkle of Parmesan to add a cheesy, golden topping. The combination of textures and flavors in every bite makes lasagna a beloved dish for family dinners, gatherings, or special occasions.

What makes lasagna truly versatile is the ability to customize it to suit different tastes and dietary needs. Vegetarians can enjoy a hearty vegetable lasagna filled with zucchini, spinach, and mushrooms, while those seeking a lighter option might opt for a white lasagna with chicken and Alfredo sauce. Whether served fresh out of the oven or reheated the next day, lasagna retains its delightful layers of flavor and comforting warmth, making it a timeless favorite that brings people together around the dinner table.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (12 sheets)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (1 pound)
  • Crushed tomatoes (1 can, 28 ounces)
  • omato paste (2 tablespoons)
  • Onion (1, finely chopped)
  • Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Ricotta cheese (2 cups)
  • Mozzarella cheese (3 cups, shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (3/4 cup, grated)
  • Egg (1)
  • Olive oil (1 tablespoon)
  • Dried basil and oregano (1 teaspoon each)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare Meat Sauce:

    Sauté chopped onion and garlic in olive oil. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.

  2. Mix Cheese Filling:

    Combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

  3. Cook Noodles:

    Boil lasagna noodles (if not oven-ready) until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  4. Assemble:

    Layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella. Top with Parmesan.

  5. Bake:

    Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake another 15–20 minutes until bubbly and golden.

  6. Rest & Serve:

    Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
